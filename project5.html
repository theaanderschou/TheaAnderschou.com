Project 5: Tiger Animation

The task was to create an animation. I chose to make a frame-by-frame animation on Adobe Animate. I made a quick stop motion video using the app we used in the Stop Motion project in the Introweek. I did this to check if there was a good flow in the movement of my frame illustrations.

I chose to use an iPad app called “Procreate” to create each frame illustration with details. This also made it possible for me to work in layers and use a great variation of brushes and colors. Yet I’m really looking forward to dig into some Adobe Illustrator in the nearest futur! Below you will see the timelapse of the work I made in Procreate on frame number 6.

Visit my blog if you want to see the frame-by-frame storyboard drawn with pencil, my stopmotion video or my timelapse video.
