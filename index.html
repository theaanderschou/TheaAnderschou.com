About Me

My name is Thea Krogh Anderschou. I’m 26 years of age and currently studying to become a multimedia design student at UCL, University College Lillebælt, in Odense. Before this, I have studied Law for approximately 2 years at SDU, University of Southern Denmark. I have always been a creative individual and I realized that I wouldn’t thrive in the law world as much as I did in the creative world, which is why I chose to change my education. Yet I still love economics, law, stocks, marketing and all that stuff. Before this I studied at the business high school in Odense with Chinese as my third language and became a talentstudent there. Beside my education I spend a lot of time on my family, boyfriend and friends. Especially my ink art instagram account @stormyandink is something I spend a lot of time on. I started @stormyandink during law school to keep something creative in my life. I use it to develop my skills in both ink art, and every technique linked to this. I also use it to develop my skill on marketing and branding on social media, which I believe is a big asset these days. And it is my plan to make it into an even bigger income in the future, in which I want to apply the skills I develop during my multimedia design education. E.g. I want to create posters, phone cases, stickers and maybe even clothing at some point.

I always work hard and does a great work in the projects I take on. I always strive to learn new things and become better at the things I do and work with. It’s very rare that I sit still and do nothing, because I love to work and develop myself.

Feel free to contact me if you have any questions for me!
