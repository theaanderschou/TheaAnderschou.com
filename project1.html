Project 1: Photo assignment

Below you will see my latest assignment. The assignment was to shoot different types of photos; Landscape, product and portraits. After doing this we had to edit them to improve the quality and learn different aspects of photo editing.

Every picture on the left is the after-editing picture and the picture on the right is the before-editing picture.

You can read more about the assignment and see a few more pictures of the process on my Process Portfolio by pressing the button below.

Read more about it in my blog

Bøjestræde, Faaborg, Denmark

This landscape photo was shot in Faaborg in december on my iPhone X. I saturated the picture and gave it warmer tones to make it more appealing. The photo was edited in Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop.

Nakkebølle Fjord, Faaborg, Denmark

This landscape photo was shot in Faaborg in december on my iPhone X. I saturated the picture and gave it warmer tones and more contrast to make it more appealing. The photo was edited in Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop.

My Abelstedt Fingering in Ringbox

This product photo was shot in UCL Medialab with a Sony Alpha A7 Mark 3. I saturated the picture and gave it warmer tones to make it more appealing. I gave the photo more height and removed the horizontal lines behind the ringbox to make it look more professional. I also used healing to remove the scratches on the glass the ringbox stands on. The photo was edited in Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop.

Abelstedt Fingerring on Finger

This product photo was shot in UCL Medialab with a Sony Alpha A7 Mark 3. I saturated the picture and gave it warmer tones to make it more appealing. I also removed little hairs from my finger, skin that looked dry and flaws in the cuticles. The photo was edited in Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop.

Me and the Sony Alpha A7 Mark 3

This portrait photo was shot in UCL Medialab with a Sony Alpha A7 Mark 3. I tried to soften my dark roots, but this doesn't look that great. I saturated the picture and gave it warmer tones to make it more appealing. The photo was edited in Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop.

Silhouette in colors

This portrait photo was shot in UCL Medialab with a Sony Alpha A7 Mark 3. I exposed and saturated the picture and gave it warmer tones to make it more appealing and my face became more visible. The photo was edited in Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop.

Silhouette in Black/White

This portrait photo was shot in UCL Medialab with a Sony Alpha A7 Mark 3. I tried to make B/W silhouette photo and exposed myself to make the photo more appealing and nicer to focus on for the eyes. I also cropped the photo to get closer and give it more of a close-up effect. The photo was edited in Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop.
