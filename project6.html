Project 6: Design a Dating App

My class got the task to create a Dating App Design in groups. We started doing a brainstorm to decide which target group we wanted to pursue. We chose to create a Dating App to people with a high IQ, who wanted to find a partner that could match them on their high IQ levels. Afterwards we discussed how to make our design appealing to our target group and found a brandname that we though would be fitting for this specific target group.
We spend most of the day developing the design. But we tested the UI on two of the other groups and our two teachers Anni and Andrea.
We used the testing and the feedback from our test users to edit and develop the app further. You can watch the video presentation on the right.
