Project 4: Redesign a website

For "Project 1" we were given the task to redesign a website in our groups. My group chose the website Jatoba.dk, which is a dog kennel for the breed Tervueren and Groenendael.

We applied models and theories from our lessons, such as N.U.G. (User test tool – “Nobanet Usability Guideline”), Think Aloud (User test tool), The C.R.A.P. Analysis (“Contrast, Repetition, Alignment and Proximity”). From these methods we were able to fin the focus point and priorities for our redesigns of the website. We prioritized the following three tasks: 1. Design and Layout, 2. Navigation and menu, 3. Contact information. But we also found the time to do our two less prioritized tasks: 1. Create new logo, and 2. Add Social Media information.

We created a new design and a new layout, added a navigation/menubar, a contact subpage with a contact form, added a new logo and added social media information on the contact subpage and in the footer. We got great feedback from our educator. Yet we had different things we could improve, such as a less text dense client report and some changes in the design of the client report. We got the grade above average.
