Project 3: Weekend with Buster (Video Editing)

Buster is my sisters dog and the "goodest" of good boys.

I created the video in Adobe Premiere Pro and I used effects such as video and audio transitions, audio from bensound.com, graphics for the intro, outdo and “starring”-part, a timelapse clip (drawing clip), a hyper lapse clip (the sunset, which I reversed to make a fake sunrise too). I used my iPhone X to film everything and a DJI OM 4 (Gimbal) I bought to make it easier to create timelapses of my drawing process etc.
